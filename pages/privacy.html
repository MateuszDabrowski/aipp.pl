Privacy

Legal stuff. Short and simple.

Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer by websites that you visit. They are widely used to make websites work and provide analytical data to the owner of the site.

Data processing

When you visit this website, I may collect some information about your activities through Google Analytics Solutions. I only collect general information such as country or city where you are located, pages visited, time spent on pages, heat-map of your activity on the site, information about the browser you are using, etc. The data collected is used in the aggregated form to improve this website and spot trends without deliberately identifying individuals.

Opt-out

Most web browsers allow some control of most cookies through the browser settings. You can also opt out Google tracking across all websites by downloading the Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on. If you have any additional questions regarding data processing on this website, feel free to send me an e‑mail.

Retention

Personal information mentioned above is aggregated and stored in a central database provided by Google Analytics Solutions and is erased on a regular, rolling basis.

Access

You are generally entitled to access personal information collected on this website and to have inaccurate data corrected or removed. In certain circumstances, you also may have the right to object for legitimate reasons to the processing or transfer of personal information. If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please write me an e‑mail explaining your request.

Disclosure

I do not disclose your personal information to any third parties except aggregated data used by Google Analytics Solutions.

Changes

I may occasionally update this Privacy Policy to add information about new tools used on the website that may have access to aggregated data.

Contact

Want to get in touch? Drop me an e‑mail or connect on LinkedIn.
